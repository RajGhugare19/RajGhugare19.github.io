I am currently working as a research intern at MILA, advised by Glen Berseth. During my undergraduate, I worked on model-based RL, advised by Ben Eysenbach. Although I graduated from VNIT Nagpur in Electronics, I mostly studied machine learning and math from MIT OCW and NPTEL during that time.

My research studies reinforcement learning in general, aiming at building autonomous agents that learn a model of their environment to exhibit behaviors that are useful. My main research interests in reinforcement learning are model-based RL, hierarchical RL, representation and abstractions in RL. The developments in specialised topics like unsupervised RL, continual learning and generalisation also interest me. I am happy to work on these specialisations in the context of supervised learning as well.
